Our Journey

  • 2002
    Launch of WebSphere
    WebSphere, our flagship product, was introduced as a comprehensive suite of software tools designed to help businesses build, deploy, and manage web applications. It set new standards in web technology with its powerful integration and scalability features.
  • 2007
    Introduction of SiteBuilder
    SiteBuilder revolutionized website creation by allowing users to build stunning, functional websites with a user-friendly drag-and-drop interface. This innovation made web development accessible to everyone, from small businesses to large enterprises.
  • 2012
    Launch of CloudSync
    CloudSync was a game-changer in data management, offering seamless synchronization of files across multiple devices and platforms. It provided robust cloud storage solutions with advanced security features and collaborative tools.
  • 2020
    Launch of DevHub
    DevHub marked our expansion into the development community with a comprehensive platform for software development. It integrates development tools, version control, and collaborative features to enhance productivity and streamline workflows for developers worldwide.
